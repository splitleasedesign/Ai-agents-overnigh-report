The FAB is positioned in the "Easy Zone" for right-handed users:

  • OK Zone: Middle area - secondary actions

Summary: Mobile-Critical Checklist

Every interactive element meets 44x44px minimum. Checkboxes, buttons, links, calendar cells all compliant.

3-Tap Rule

Primary actions (Pay, Sign, Accept/Decline) reachable in 3 taps or fewer from any state.

Week Strip Calendar

Replaced month grid with swipeable week strip. 44x44px cells guarantee accurate date selection.

Bottom Sheets

All expandable content uses swipe-dismissible bottom sheets instead of inline accordions.

Cards over Tables

Payment tables transform to 120px minimum-height cards below 768px breakpoint.
