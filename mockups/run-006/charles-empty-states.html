5. First DCT Onboarding Tooltip

Educational tooltip explaining how date changes work. Appears on first click of "Request Date Change" button.

📅 Request a Date Change
Life happens! Here's how it works:
  1. 1 Select the dates you need to swap
  2. 2 Add a brief note explaining why (helps approval!)
  3. 3 Your host gets notified and has 48h to respond
  4. 4 If approved, your schedule updates automatically
Tips for approval:
  • Request early - more notice = higher approval
  • Offer alternatives if your first choice doesn't work
  • Be specific about the reason (work trip, family event, etc.)
